Patient List

The Patient List section allows you to view and manage all registered patients within the system. You can easily search for patients by name or ID, access their overview details, and edit or progress their care plans.

  • Search patients by name/ID: Quickly find a specific patient by entering their name or ID in the search bar. This feature helps healthcare professionals retrieve patient information efficiently.
  • Patients Overview: View a comprehensive list of all registered patients. Access each patientâ€™s profile containing essential information like medical history and contact details.
  • Patient Profiles: View and edit detailed patient information to ensure that records are accurate and up-to-date, enabling personalized care.
  • Edit: Modify patient information such as contact details or medical history, ensuring that records reflect changes in conditions or treatment plans.
  • Progress: Track patient progress through their care plan and update status or notes to reflect changes in their condition.

Add New Patient

The Add New User section allows you to add new patient and do all the registion process through the appliction.

  • First Name: Enter the user's first name for identification and communication.
  • Middle Name: Provide the user's middle name to differentiate between similar names.
  • Last Name: Enter the user's last name for formal identification and record-keeping.
  • Date of Birth: Input the user's date of birth for age verification and demographic purposes.
  • Medicare Number: Include the user's Medicare number for health insurance and medical records.
  • Western Affairs Number: Add the Western Affairs number if applicable for administrative purposes.

Nurse Roster

The Nurse Roster section allows you to manage nurse tasks and track the progress of the nurse through the application.

  • Managing Nurse Rosters: This feature allows administrators to create and manage nurse schedules, ensuring proper staffing for each shift.
  • Viewing Nurse Progress: Track the progress of assigned tasks, monitor nurse performance, and ensure quality care for patients through real-time updates.
  • Additional Features: Access advanced functionalities like shift swapping, leave management, and automated notifications to streamline nurse operations.

Troubleshooting

Having trouble with the system? Check out our troubleshooting guide below to resolve common issues.

  • Login Issues:

    Ensure you have the correct username and password. If you forget your password, use the "Forgot Password" link.

  • User Management Errors:

    Check for correct user details and roles.

  • Activity Data Not Loading:

    Refresh the page or check your internet connection.

  • Device Compatibility:

    Verify that the device used to track activity is compatible with the system and properly paired.
