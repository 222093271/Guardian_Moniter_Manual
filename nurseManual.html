Welcome to Nurse User Manual

Patient List View

The Patient List section allows you to view and manage all registered patients within the system. You can easily search for patients by name or ID, access their overview details, and edit or progress their care plans.

  • Patient Profiles: Each patient entry displays key information like name, age, gender, and ID number.
  • Searching for Patients: Use the search bar at the top of the page to search by patient name or patient ID number.
  • Viewing Patient Information: The patient profile will display basic information such as age, gender, and patient ID.
  • View Health Details:

    Access the patient's medical history, vital signs, allergies, and other health information.

  • View Tasks:

    View assigned tasks for the patient, such as medication administration or wound care.

  • View Daily Report:

    Access a comprehensive summary of the patient's daily care, including notes, vital signs, and treatments.

Patient Daily Report

The Patient Daily Report section allows the nurse to manage the patient report on the daily basics. Nurse can write the note about the progress or the anything for the patient.

  • Reported By and Report Date: A text field where the caretaker's name or ID is automatically populated. A dropdown menu to select the day, month, and year for the report.
  • Progress Notes: A text box for the caretaker to enter detailed notes about the patient's condition, observations, and daily activities
  • Alert: A section for selecting urgent situations or required levels of care. Urgent Medical Attention(For immediate medical needs) Requires Hospitalisation(For hospitalization) Requires hourly monitoring(For close observation) Regular Monitoring(3 times a day For routine monitoring)

Troubleshooting

Having trouble with the system? Check out our troubleshooting guide below to resolve common issues.

  • Login Issues:

    Ensure you have the correct username and password. If you forget your password, use the "Forgot Password" link.

  • User Management Errors:

    Check for correct user details and roles.

  • Activity Data Not Loading:

    Refresh the page or check your internet connection.
